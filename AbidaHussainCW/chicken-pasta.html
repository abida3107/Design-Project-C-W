Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken breasts
  • 250g of your favorite pasta (such as penne, fettuccine, or rotini).
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes, halved.
  • 1 cup of baby spinach or arugula leaves.
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream or half-and-half.
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese.
  • 1 teaspoon of dried Italian herbs (oregano, basil, thyme).
  • Seasoning: Garlic powder + Paprika + Italian Seasoning.
  • Salt and black pepper to taste.
  • Red pepper flakes (optional, for a kick of spice).
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish.
